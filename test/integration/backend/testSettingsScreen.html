Amazon S3 Protected Content

Secured Amazon S3 URLs are recommended for member-only sections of the site. We check the video length and make sure the link expiration time is big enough for the video to buffer properly.

If you use a cache plugin (such as Hyper Cache, WP Super Cache or W3 Total Cache), we recommend that you set the "Default Expiration Time" to twice as much as your cache timeout and check "Force the default expiration time". That way the video length won't be accounted and the video source URLs in your cached pages won't expire. Read more in the Using Amazon S3 secure content in FV Flowplayer guide.
